ABOUT US


Association of Technology Students is an academic organization in Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Maragondon Branch. Association of Technology Students composed of technology students from the two Diploma Program of Polytechnic University of the Philippines; Diploma in Office Management Technology and Diploma in Infomation and Communication Technology.

Association of Technology Students conducts different events and activities related to the technology and development to help students from Association of Technology Students develop their academic skills and leadership skills.
